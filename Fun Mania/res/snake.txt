Description :
Here you have to just eat the candy and make
yourself as big as possible without eating
yourself.
In the two player option you have to eat the
candy and get bigger than the other snake
and then you can eat him to win the game.

Controls :
First Snake :
UP ARROW key to move up.
LEFT ARROW key to move left.
DOWN ARROW key to move down.
RIGHT ARROW key to move right.

Second Snake :
W key to move up.
D key to move right.
S key to move down.
A key o move left.
